About

CodeMacabre builds websites, open source web applications and data visualisations themed around death, mortality and other aspects of the macabre.

Why? Because death is something we all experience, regardless of our gender, sexuality, race, mental or physical ableness, wealth, etc. It is the ultimate in inclusivity and by understanding our equality in mortality and respecting others accordingly, we can build better lives for all.

I intend to begin with the web.
